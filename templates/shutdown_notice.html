SwitchSnap is shutting down on 31th of January 2021

SwitchSnap shutting down on 31th of January 2021 and all data is to be deleted.

If you wish to obtain a copy of your user data. Please contact me via email or twitter (@olafyxyz)

With Nintendo releasing a new update in November of 2020. Transfer between switch and mobile devices was made significantly easier.

Switch has historically been lacking proper online services. One of the reason I created SwitchSnap is to allow easy transfer of screenshots. Although Nintendo is still not offering a "screenshot cloud" I have in vision. This is the first step towards a online infrastructure that's expected in 2021.

The current model (using twitter) of SwitchSnap is significantly outshined by the official solution and not up to the vision I had in mind. It is clear that SwitchSnap can not compete under current circumstances. That's not to say that SwitchSnap won't return in the future with a better service model.

Developing SwitchSnap have taught me invaluable knowledge and experience. I am extremely thankful for every user of SwitchSnap for giving me and SwitchSnap a chance.

Olaf Yang, Developer
